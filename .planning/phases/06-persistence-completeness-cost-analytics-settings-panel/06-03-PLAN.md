<objective>
Instrument the cost-capture pipeline end-to-end on the backend. `query_model` parses `usage.cost` + `usage.cost_details.upstream_inference_cost` (field paths verified in Plan 01) from every successful OpenRouter response and returns them as a `cost` sub-dict. Callers in `council.py` and `research_strategy.py` accumulate per-stage sums, then pack the consolidated `metadata.cost = {stage1, stage2, stage3, stage4, total, upstream_total, currency}` blob into the same opaque `metadata` dict that PERS-01 just landed.

Purpose: COST-01 is the single source-of-truth for cost data in the system. Every downstream surface (MessageHeader cost line in Plan 04, `/api/stats/cost` aggregation in Plan 04, eventual analytics) reads from this blob. Getting the shape right here is more important than any UI polish ŌĆö the UI is replaceable, persisted metadata is not.

Output: All three streaming branches (fresh, quality_research, critique) persist `metadata.cost` on every new assistant message. The dict shape is the locked contract documented in CONTEXT.md and RESEARCH.md.
</objective>
